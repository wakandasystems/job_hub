📋 Key Assumptions Underpinning These Numbers
  • Exchange rate of $1 = K19.00 (May 2026 mid-market rate). Kwacha has strengthened in 2025–26; this is a realistic near-term rate.
  • Zambia-first pricing used for Year 1. Prices are calibrated to local employer purchasing power — not imported USD pricing.
  • Year 1 assumes Zambia only. Year 2 adds SA and Zimbabwe. Year 3 adds Nigeria or Kenya + 1–2 others.
  • Conservative = minimal traction, 5–10 paying employers, low traffic. Realistic = steady execution, 30–50 employers, 20K+ monthly visitors. Strong = high execution, 100+ employers, 80K+ monthly visitors.
  • Managed Recruitment figures assume 15–20% success fee on placed salaries. Junior role = K380,000 salary → K57,000–K76,000 fee. Senior = K1.5M salary → K225,000–K300,000 fee.
  • These are gross revenue estimates, not profit. Deduct hosting (~K1,900/mo), payment gateway fees (~3%), staff, and marketing costs to arrive at net.
  • No advertising spend assumed. Growth via SEO, direct employer outreach, and word of mouth — realistic for a bootstrapped start.
